As the undergraduate TA for AEP 3330: Mechanical Particles and Solid Bodies, I was responsible for verifying the solution sets for homework and grading weekly assignments. Additionally, I addressed students’ inquiries related to homework and exam preparation.

Intern, Aodong Electronic Technology

Dec 2021 - Apr 2022

In this internship experience, I developed an efficient conversion of frequency stability between the time domain and the frequency domain. Utilizing this conversion method, I implemented a MATLAB-based simulation program to model the impact of phase noise on velocity measurement precision. Experiments were conducted with TT&C systems across a range of carrier frequencies. It was found that when measuring the velocity of long-range targets, high carrier frequencies should be used to reduce phase noise related measurement errors.

I placed an emphasis on teaching advanced problem-solving techniques. Additionally, I held weekly sessions for the entire class to provide homework guidance and introduce effective strategies for exam preparation.
