About me

I am Sam Gou, a senior undergraduate student in the College of Engineering at Cornell University. My major is Applied & Engineering Physics.

My research interests lie in quantum information processing (QIP) and high-energy physics. I want to continue my research in QIP in graduate school. When I'm not doing research, you can find me cooking, reading, and playing ice hockey.
